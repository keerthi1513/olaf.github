About

Nomadic Tribes, created by MakePulse in 2020, is an interactive web experience exploring ancient and modern nomadic cultures through rich storytelling and stunning visuals.

Background

The project leverages WebGL, Three.js, and HTML/CSS/JavaScript for 3D environments, with tools like Blender and Shaders (GLSL) for lifelike visuals. Node.js powers the back-end, while hosting is managed on AWS or Google Cloud.
