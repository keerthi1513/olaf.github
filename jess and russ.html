All About the Website

The website jessandruss.us is an interactive online wedding invitation created by designers Jessica Hische and Russ Maschmeyer for their 2012 wedding. It narrates their love story through a timeline enriched with custom illustrations, animations, and engaging storytelling, offering guests an immersive experience into their journey as a couple.
